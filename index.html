  • Aboriginal Networks and Websites

    Aboriginal Networks and Websites is a Canadian organization that helps the Aboriginal Canadian community by providing inexpensive websites and internet marketing support. Our goal is to help native Canadians and aboriginal businesses reach and exceed their business goals. Internet marketing can open up markets and opportunities that only a few years ago were out of reach.

Can you change the world?
We are inspiring people to get inspired to change their future!

‘A person can change his future by merely changing his attitude.’
—Oprah Winfrey
  1. Care. Show a person that you genuinely care about them and you will be able to inspire them.
  2. Enthusiasm. Be enthusiastic. It's contagious.
  3. Trust. Trust takes a long time to build, but it can be destroyed overnight.
  4. Be Positive. If you don't have anything nice to say don't say anything at all.
  5. Build People Up. Small compliments build self-esteem. Believing in people can work miracles.
  6. Listen. Actively listen and comprehend what's being said and not said to fully understand the meaning.
  7. Create possibilities. Explore alternative thoughts and ideas by challenging your own beliefs.
